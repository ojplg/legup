LegUp is a tool for grading Illinois State Legislators. Users can create Report Cards that contain lists of bills and their desired outcomes. Those bills are then compared against the voting and sponsorship records of Illinois House and Senate members and grades are calculated accordingly.

LegUp has three major capabilities:

  1. Importing data from the Illinois State General Assembly web site.
  2. Browsing information about bill voting records of the assembly members, either by bill or member.
  3. Creating Report Cards and calculating grades.
